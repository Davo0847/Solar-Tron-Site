Specialist Services

Solar Asset Inspections

Comprehensive O&M inspection services covering string fault identification, module replacement, and inverter fault diagnostics. We support EPCs and asset owners with targeted repairs, performance optimisation, and reduced downtime.

Asset Health Checks

Structured annual inspection programmes designed to identify early degradation, mitigate warranty risks, and maintain peak performance. Prevention is better than cure.

Serial Number Traceability

We create a digital twin of the DC field by linking modules to location and test data, enabling precise defect tracking and strong warranty support.

Post-Shipment Module Testing

ISO-sampled EL, IV and electrical testing to identify manufacturing defects and transport damage before installation. Provides EPCs with clear, lender-grade evidence for acceptance and warranty claims using a Pasan Avalon Nexun mobile lab.
