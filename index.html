Solar Asset Inspections

Comprehensive O&M inspection services designed to identify and resolve string faults, module failures, and inverter performance issues. Our engineers deliver detailed fault diagnostics, targeted repair strategies, and module replacement services to ensure optimal system performance. We support EPCs and asset owners by reducing downtime, improving yield, and protecting long-term asset value.

Asset Health Checks

Proactive annual inspection and testing programmes designed to maintain solar asset performance and mitigate long-term risk. Our health checks identify early-stage degradation, warranty risks, and performance inefficiencies. Prevention is better than cure — our structured approach helps asset owners stay ahead of failures, protect investments, and maximise lifecycle returns.

About Solar Tron

Solar Tron Ltd is an independent specialist provider of utility-scale solar inspection and technical assurance services. The company is led by senior engineers with over 15 years of hands-on experience in the delivery, inspection, and quality assurance of large-scale solar PV projects.

Solar Tron’s role is observational and supportive, aligned with EPC delivery requirements and focused on identifying mechanical installation workmanship risks, including torque application where instructed.

Scope includes:

  • Module installation quality, alignment, and consistency
  • Clamp positioning and compliance with manufacturer requirements
  • Mechanical interface and structural integrity checks
  • Identification of stress, distortion, or installation defects
  • Tracker and fixed-tilt system workmanship verification
  • Targeted torque verification where specified
  • Mechanical-electrical interface observations
  • Identification of systemic workmanship issues

Mission Statement:
Our mission is to deliver world-class solar inspection and testing services that uphold the highest standards of safety, quality, and sustainability. We support the transition to clean energy by ensuring solar assets operate efficiently and reliably throughout their lifecycle.
