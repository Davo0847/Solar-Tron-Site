Specialist Services

Solar Asset Inspections

Comprehensive O&M inspection services covering string fault identification, module replacement, and inverter fault diagnostics. We support EPCs and asset owners with targeted repairs, performance optimisation, and reduced downtime across utility-scale assets.

Asset Health Checks

Structured annual inspection programmes designed to identify early-stage degradation, mitigate warranty risks, and maintain peak system performance. Prevention is better than cure — our proactive approach protects long-term asset value and maximises lifecycle returns.

Serial Number Traceability

We create a digital twin of the DC field by linking every module to its exact location. By associating EL, IV, thermal, and visual data to serial numbers, we enable precise defect tracking, batch analysis, and robust warranty claim support — removing ambiguity in manufacturer negotiations.

Post-Shipment Module Testing

Post-shipment testing isolates manufacturing and logistics liability before installation. Using ISO 2859-1 sampling, we test representative modules with EL imaging, IV curve analysis, flash testing, and electrical verification to detect micro-cracks, transport damage, and latent defects. Results are analysed at cell level and aggregated into batch statistics, providing EPCs with clear defect profiling, risk visibility, and lender-grade evidence for acceptance, remediation, or warranty claims. Delivered using a market-leading Pasan Avalon Nexun mobile lab.
